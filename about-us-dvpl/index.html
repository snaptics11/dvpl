About DVPL

We are not just your internet provider.
We are your business partner.

D-Atum Vilcom Private Limited (DVPL) is a ‘A’ category Internet Service Provider (ISP) certified by the Department of Telecom (DoT) India. Driven by technology and powered by efficiency, we provide reliable, high-speed and secure internet across several states in India. Our innovative and practical approach to optimizing connectivity for individuals and businesses helped us formulate services and plans that cater to all.

Our focus on quality and reliable service enabled us to rise through the ranks consistently. In an increasingly digital world, we want businesses and individuals to harness technology to innovate products and services and grow with reliable, high-speed internet connections.

We ensure consistent quality in the services we deliver to our clients. We work hard, listen to our clients and work with them to provide the expected solutions. DVPL has grown many folds in the last few years because we adhere to our core principles of reliability, integrity and transparency.

Through our network, we wish to perpetuate and deliver end-to-end internet solutions. With expertise garnered over the years, we are on the brink of establishing ourselves as a forerunner among Internet Service Providers.
